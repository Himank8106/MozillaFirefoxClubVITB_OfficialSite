MOZILLA FIREFOX CLUB

Mozilla Firefox Club-VIT Is The Club For Mozilla Firefox Open-Source Community Enthusiasts At VIT University, Bhopal, To Promote Web Development And Open Source Technologies In The University.

ABOUT US

The Mozilla Firefox club at VIT Bhopal is a student-led community of individuals who are passionate about technology and are committed to creating an enormous open-source community. The club aims at constructing a community of talented and creative problem-solvers who work with each other to bring out exciting innovative solutions. Bringing exciting events to life and making a community that grows together in all aspects for a better tech person The club provides learning opportunities to supplement personal growth and strengthen leadership qualities among learners. Gathering like minded individuals and letting them achieve their potential through collective effort is the main motto of the club. We, at Mozilla Firefox, strive to give an opportunity to inspire and get inspired.

OUR AIM

The Mozilla Firefox VIT Bhopal Is An Interactive Club Targeting Web Development. As We Know That Web Development Has A High Scope In The Upcoming Times And Our College Has Many Talented Students. Most Students Don't Get Appropriate Guidance And Opportunity To Outcast Their Skills And Advance Their Career In The Field Of Web Development. Our Club Aims To Reach Out To Them, Help And Support Them Regarding The Same. Our Club Will Help Individuals To Get Their Correct Domain So That They Can Work Precisely In Their Field And Grab The Best Placement. Our Club Will Work As The Voice Of Students In The Mozilla Community And Will Give Them Some Expert Support And Opportunity For Their Growth.

Our Domains

We believe in hands on learning and freedom of work. Hence all of our core committee members.
are provided with selection of five domains to find where their passion lies.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

This domain provides students with a platform where they are encouraged to discover, explore and learn more about technology.

EVENT MANAGEMENT

This domain provides students with a platform where we bring out ideas to life through engaging and impactful events.

PR and OUTREACH

This domain provides students with a platform where they can connect with the community and build relationships with the outside world.

DESIGN and VIDEOGRAPHY

This domain promotes and instigates curiosity and interest regarding graphics and media design and overall enriching the creativeness of the club.

SOCIAL MEDIA

This group of students specialize in the digital media world and explore their creativity in the various technical aspects of the media world.

CONTENT

This domain provides exclusive exposure to students in developing intellectual writing capabilities and provides authentic content for the club.
